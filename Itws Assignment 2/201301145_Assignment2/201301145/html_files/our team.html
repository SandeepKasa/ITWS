The PEARL MANAGEMENT TEAM

Image About
Heiko Gloge: Managing Director and Partner Mr Gloge is a pillar of the international thin client community. After starting with Melchers in 1988, he was made General Manager of their NetCom division, which in 2001 became the independent daughter company, PEARL Technology . He was one of the founders of the European Thin Client Forum in 2002 and currently is Chairman of the Thin Client & Server Based Computing group in Germany highly influential IT industry association, BITKOM. A native of North Germany, Mr. Gloge originally studied Computing at different institutions.
“We are on a mission at PEARL to change our industry and help our customers reap the benefits that this new world of network computing grow will deliver.”
Nicolas Helms: Managing Director and Partner Nicolas Helms is Executive Partner at Melchers Group, parent company of PEARL Technology . Originally from Bremen, he has worked in six different countries on four continents, where he has gained extensive experience in international business. In 1994 he was named Director of Melchers Hong Kong. Mr. Helms has been Managing Director at C. Melchers Co. KG since 2000. He was one of two directors who founded PEARL Technology GmbH as a Melchers subsidiary in 2001. Nicolas Helms holds a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) from the University of Zurich, Switzerland, and a Master of Business Administration (MBA) from Boston University, USA. "I see it as a great challenge to establish PEARL as our own leading international IT brand."
Dirk Dördelmann: Managing Director Mr Doerdelmann is one of the industry’s most highly qualified engineering managers with more than 15 years practical experience in server-based computing and development of both thin clients and terminals. He joined PEARL in 1991 taking responsibility for the technical department from service to support. Then for a period of three years he joined OBO Bettermann to gain additional project management experience in the electrical industry. In 2001 he returned to PEARL to set-up, structure, organize and manage PEARL's world class German engineering teams for product management, R & D, and quality assurance in the Augsburg branch office. Mr. Doerdelmann has professional qualifications in both electro-technique and in commerce. “As a team we develop innovative PEARL products and solutions, which are always characterized as offering our customers a significant competitive advantage through technology, quality and practicality.”
Andreas Schönduve: Managing Director Mr Schönduve joined PEARL in 2006. By introducing permanent, long-view cost control systems, implementing dynamic cash management and increasing overall efficiency, he has dramatically improved the Finance, Administration and Control department. In doing so, he has made it possible for PEARL’s top management to make clear, well-informed decisions on company policies even in these uncertain times. For over 20 years, Andreas Schönduve has been active in international business on five continents. During this time, he was also able to complete a program of study at the well-respected German Foreign Trade and Transportation Academy (Deutsche Außenhandels- und Verkehrsakademie DAV) in Bremen, Germany. “With all our interest in numbers and calculations, the development of standards and the adherence to established processes, we mustn’t ever forget that real people are behind the numbers and that only a portion of our work and jobs can be done by machines and programs. The constant willingness to act in a flexible, innovative and targeted manner as well as the high priority that PEARL gives to fairness and openness in its daily dealings with its suppliers, customers and employees have made me very fond of this company, right from the very start.”
Erhard Behnke Director Sales Mr Behnke is an experienced international sales leader with a proven track record in channel and key account sales in the server-based computing and thin clients market. Since 1984, he has gained a wealth of IT sales experience and prior to joining PEARL was Manager Brand Marketing EMEA at Novell where he successfully developed a strong channel through the introduction of joint technology products that addressed customer and market needs. A German based international system house benefited from his skills for over ten years as he managed and was operationally responsible implementing technical training facilities and consulting services within the Microsoft authorized and Citrix authorized markets. Mr. Behnke holds degrees in electro-technique, computer science and information technology from University of Bremen, Germany. "I work to ensure PEARL attains market leadership in the medium-term, based on the consistent and ongoing customer satisfaction gained through IGEL products and services."
